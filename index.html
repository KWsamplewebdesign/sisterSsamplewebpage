Our Story

Founded with a passion for traditional craftsmanship, Knot & Thread brings the timeless art of knotting to modern homes. Each piece is carefully handcrafted using premium threads and ancient knotting techniques passed down through generations.

What began as a hobby in a small studio has grown into a beloved brand that celebrates the beauty of handmade art. We believe that every knot tells a story and every piece carries the warmth of human touch.

Our commitment to quality means we never rush the creative process. Each design is thoughtfully planned, meticulously crafted, and finished with attention to every detail. When you choose Knot & Thread, you're not just buying decor – you're bringing home a piece of art made with intention and love.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order custom designs?

Absolutely! We love creating custom pieces tailored to your specific needs. Whether you want a particular color scheme, size, or design element, simply reach out to us through WhatsApp with your requirements, and our team will work with you to bring your vision to life.

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple! Browse our collections, select the piece you love, and click the "Order on WhatsApp" button. This will open a WhatsApp chat with a pre-filled message about your interest. You can then discuss details, customization options, and payment directly with our team.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Since each piece is handmade to order, production typically takes 5-7 business days. Custom designs may require additional time depending on complexity. Once crafted, we ship via express delivery, which usually takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order is on its way.

Can I choose specific colors for my piece?

Yes! We offer a range of color options for most of our pieces. You can choose from our standard color palette or provide specific color references for custom orders. Simply share your color preferences when ordering through WhatsApp, and we'll do our best to match your vision.

How is my order packaged?

We take great care in packaging your handcrafted pieces. Each item is wrapped in premium materials, placed in a sturdy box, and finished with elegant wrapping. If it's a gift, we can include a personalized message and gift tag. Our packaging is designed to protect your piece while creating a beautiful unboxing experience.

Ready to Order Your Handmade Favorite?

Connect with us directly on WhatsApp for personalized assistance, custom designs, and quick responses to all your queries. Our team is excited to help you find or create the perfect handcrafted piece for your space.
